PATHANAMTHITTA



The Pilgrim Capital of Kerala

In this idyllic realm, one can lose themselves amidst the mesmerizing dance of vibrant flora and fauna. Its tranquil hill stations, such as Charalkunnu, cast a spell with panoramic vistas of rolling hills, and the Palaruvi Waterfall, akin to a celestial cascade, bestows ethereal serenity. Gavi, a hidden gem, whispers tales of eco-adventures in its dense wilderness. Pathanamthitta, with its rich tapestry of life, where the Periyar Lake reflects the regal grace of elephants, embodies the essence of a pristine sanctuary. This district is a treasure trove of nature's wonders, inviting discerning travelers to immerse themselves in its exquisite beauty. It is an enchanting district in the verdant embrace of Kerala, beckons with the alluring splendor of its emerald landscapes.



Places to visit




Gavi

Surrounded by lush forests, it offers visitors the chance to engage in activities such as trekking, bird-watching, and camping, providing a tranquil escape into the heart of nature. Gavi is a peaceful and off-the-beaten-path destination that allows travelers to experience the untouched wilderness of Kerala.

Aranmula Vallam Kali

A grand spectacle featuring long, gracefully crafted snake boats and is an integral part of the cultural and religious traditions associated with the Aranmula Parthasarathy Temple festival. The race attracts numerous participants and spectators who gather to witness the thrilling and colorful event that showcases the rich heritage of Kerala's boat races.

Kottavanchi Boating

During this unique boating experience, visitors travel in traditional Kottavanchi boats, which are wooden country boats. These boats allow tourists to explore the serene waters of Periyar Lake, offering a tranquil and immersive way to observe the lush landscapes and wildlife of Periyar National Park in a traditional, eco-friendly manner.

Konni Forest Reserve

A pristine natural habitat located in the Pathanamthitta district of Kerala, India. It is renowned for its serene and lush surroundings, and it houses an elephant training center where visitors can observe and interact with these majestic creatures and learn about their mahouts (trainers). The reserve also offers elephant rides and is a fascinating destination for those interested in wildlife and the rich cultural heritage associated with these gentle giants.

Perunthenaruvi Waterfalls

A stunning natural attraction known for its cascading waters surrounded by lush greenery. The falls are a popular destination for picnics, nature enthusiasts, and those seeking a serene and refreshing escape amidst the beauty of the Western Ghats.

Periyar National Park

A biodiverse sanctuary known for its dense forests and remarkable wildlife, including elephants, tigers, and various bird species. The park offers a unique boat safari on Periyar Lake, allowing visitors to witness wildlife up close in its natural habitat, making it a must-visit destination for nature enthusiasts.
